Thailand Lamp & Lighting Market

CY2024 – CY2025 · International Management Consulting Study · 45–60 Minute Paid Consultation

About the Project

An analyst at an international consulting firm is conducting research on Thailand's Lamp & Lighting market for CY2024 and CY2025. The objective is to understand overall market size and structure, including organized vs unorganized segments, LED vs non-LED adoption, channel dynamics across B2C and B2B, and competitive positioning of major lighting players operating in Thailand.

Scope of Discussion
  • Total Lamp & Lighting market size in Thailand, including the split between organized and unorganized segments.
  • LED vs non-LED penetration for lamps, and the overall mix between lamps and lighting fixtures.
  • Performance of the organized market across B2C (Modern Trade vs General Trade — urban and rural) and B2B (residential, commercial, industrial, hospitality, sport, and infrastructure).
  • Market share estimates for key players for CY2024 and CY2025.
  • Key drivers behind market and share shifts in CY2025, including channel dynamics and sector-specific demand trends.
